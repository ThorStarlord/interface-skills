# Brief: Notification Settings

## 1. Goal

Allow users to configure their email and in-app notification preferences from a single settings screen.

## 2. Primary user

Authenticated end user. Non-technical. Uses the product daily.

## 3. Primary action

Enable or disable notification categories.

## 4. Secondary actions

- Set notification frequency (immediate / daily digest / weekly).
- Save preferences.

## 5. Why it matters

Notification fatigue is the top reason users disable all notifications, increasing churn risk.

## 6. Success criteria

- A user can update notification preferences in under 30 seconds.
- Changes are persisted immediately (no "save" required for toggles).

## 7. Constraints

- Uses existing design system tokens.
- Mobile and desktop.

## 8. Non-goals

- Notification history or inbox.
- Push notification management (separate screen).

## 9. Open questions

None.

## 10. Assumptions

- Users are authenticated.
- At least one notification category exists in the system.
